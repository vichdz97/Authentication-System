FAQ

Why was this web application created?

As part of the Capgemini NGT Program, our team wanted to showcase how we could integrate our lessons from training and additional technologies to create a full-stack web application.

Do I have to create an account?

We highly recommend creating an account in order to access all the cool features that we have to offer.

I'm trying to create an account but there is already an existing account. What do I do?

Make sure to create a unique username and password! Remember to never share your login information with anyone.

Can I create more than one account?

Although not recommended, you can definitely create more than one account. However, make sure your login credentials are unique to each account.

How can I update my username and/or password?

For users: Access your account and click on the yellow action button . You'll then be able to update your username, password, or both! Just remember to store your new credentials in a safe place.
